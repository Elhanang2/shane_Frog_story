Frog Story

By Shane

There was a frog.

He was named Keven.

Keven lived on a lilly pad, on Fog Lake.

It was very foggy in Fog Lake.

One very foggy day Keven was looking for flys to eat.

Then he missed the lilly pad and fell in the water!

After that tons of flys came and liffed Keven out!

Keven was glald; he became freinds with the flys.

He never ate flys again.

The end
